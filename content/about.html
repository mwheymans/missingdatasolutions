My name is Martijn Heymans. I am an Associate Professor at the department of Epidemiology and Biostatistics of the Amsterdam University Medical Center (Amsterdam UMC), The Netherlands.

My main topics of interest are Missing Data, Clinical Prediction Modeling, and Machine Learning. My work consists of supervising PhD projects on methodological and clinical topics, lecturing courses and statistical consultancy.

I like to work with RStudio and try to provide solutions for applied clinical researchers. I have a background in Physical Therapy, Human Movement Sciences, Epidemiology and Medical Biostatistics.
